About Shreeji Industries

Our Story

Established in 2009 in Ahmedabad, Gujarat (India), Shreeji Industries has grown into a trusted name in the manufacturing and supply of high-quality industrial components. We specialize in a wide range of products including Submersible Spares, Automobile Bushes, Copper Rings, Thrust Plates, and Thrust Bearings.

With a commitment to excellence, all our products are manufactured using the finest raw materials and cutting-edge technology, strictly adhering to international quality standards. Our advanced production facility is equipped with modern machinery that enables us to deliver products with precision, reliability, and consistency.

At the heart of Shreeji Industries is our skilled and dedicated team. Their collaborative approach and deep industry knowledge empower us to meet diverse client needs with efficiency. We continuously invest in training and skill development programs to ensure our workforce stays ahead of industry advancements.
